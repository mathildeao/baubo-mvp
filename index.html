Meet Baubo

We are on a mission to make effective support for vulvar, vaginal and pelvic floor pain accessible to everyone.

Vulvodynia and vaginismus are often taboo, making it hard for those affected to seek support. We're breaking the silence to destigmatize these conditions, empowering women to share their stories, access effective treatments, and join a supportive community where they no longer feel isolated or misunderstood.

Our name is inspired by the Greek goddess of humor, Baubo, depicted as a walking vulva. In mythology, Baubo lifted the grieving Demeter's spirits with ribald jokes and by exposing her vulva, symbolizing the power of humor to overcome darkness.

Like our namesake, we aim to bring light to those facing vulvar, vaginal and pelvic pain.
